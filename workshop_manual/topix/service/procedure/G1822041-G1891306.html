Removal
CAUTION:

If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork, the affected area must be immediately washed down with cold water.

NOTE:

Make sure that all openings are sealed.


  1. WARNING:

    Make sure to support the vehicle with axle stands.

    Raise and support the vehicle.
  2. Remove the wheel and tire.
    Refer to: Wheel and Tire (204-04 Wheels and Tires, Removal and Installation).
  3. NOTE:

    If equipped.

    Torque: 6 Nm
  6. CAUTIONS:

    • Inspect the brake hose sealing washers for signs of damage, if damage is present discard the sealing washers.

    • Be prepared to collect escaping fluids.

    NOTE:

    Make sure that all openings are sealed.

    Remove the flexible brake hose.
Installation
  1. Inspect the caliper piston and slide pin seals for damage.
  2. Torque: 58 Nm
  3. CAUTIONS:

    • Inspect the brake hose sealing washers for signs of damage, if damage is present install new sealing washers.

    • Make sure that the brake hose is not twisted and is correctly located.

    Install the flexible brake hose.
    Torque:
    Brake pipe union 15 Nm
    Banjo bolt 32 Nm
  6. NOTE:

    If equipped.

    Torque: 6 Nm
  7. Bleed the braking system.
    Refer to: Brake System Bleeding (206-00 Brake System - General Information, General Procedures).
  8. Install the wheel and tire.
    Refer to: Wheel and Tire (204-04 Wheels and Tires, Removal and Installation).
