Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs

Rear Bumper Mounting (G1785191)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the rear bumper mounting is installed in conjunction with:

  • Rear bumper
  • Rear armature
  • Battery
  • Loadspace scuff plate trim panel
  • Loadspace trim panel
  • Auxiliary junction box (AJB)
  • Subwoofer amplifier
  • Exhaust system
  1. The rear bumper mounting is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements.For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

    • Make sure a zinc rich primer is applied to any bare metal surfaces.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Rear Armature (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Battery (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Loadspace Scuff Plate Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  9. NOTE:

    Right hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Rear Junction Box (418-00 Module Communications Network, Removal and Installation).
  10. NOTE:

    Left hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Subwoofer Amplifier (415-01 Information and Entertainment System, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Exhaust System - Vehicles Without: Diesel Exhaust Fluid (309-00B Exhaust System - INGENIUM I4 2.0L Diesel, Removal and Installation) / Exhaust System (309-00C Exhaust System - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation) / Exhaust System (309-00A Exhaust System - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  12. Release and position the wiring harness to one side.
  13. Remove the spot welds as indicated.
  14. Remove the spot welds as indicated.
  15. Carefully separate the joints and remove the rear bumper mounting.
Installation
  1. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  2. Drill holes where the metal inert gas (MIG) plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  3. Drill holes where MIG plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  4. Deburr the drilled holes.
  5. Clean and prepare the panel surfaces.
  6. Offer up the new rear bumper mounting and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  7. Install the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  8. Install the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  9. Dress the MIG plug welds.
  10. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  11. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  12. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
