Removal
NOTE:
  • This procedure contains some variation in the illustrations depending on the vehicle specification, but the essential information is always correct.
  • This procedure contains illustrations showing certain components removed to provide extra clarity.

  1. Raise and support the vehicle on a suitable 2 post lift.
    Refer to: Lifting (100-02 Jacking and Lifting, Description and Operation).
  2. Remove the air defector.
    Refer to: Engine Undershield (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  3. Remove the front halfshaft.
    Refer to: Front Halfshaft (205-04 Front Drive Halfshafts, Removal and Installation).
  4. Disconnect the wiring harness and position to one side.
  5. Remove the 3 retaining bolts and remove the starter motor bracket.
  6. CAUTION:

    Be prepared to collect escaping fluids.

    Remove the halfshaft support bracket.
  7. Discard the O-ring seal.
Installation
  1. CAUTION:

    Make sure all the mating faces are clean and free from foreign material.

    Renew the O-ring seal.
    Renew Part: Halfshaft support bracket o-ring seal Quantity: 1.
  2. Install the starter motor bracket and the 3 retaining bolts.
    Torque: 25 Nm
  3. Reposition the wiring harness and secure the connector and retaining clip.
  4. Install the halfshaft support bracket.
    Torque: 25 Nm
  5. Install the front halfshaft.
    Refer to: Front Halfshaft (205-04 Front Drive Halfshafts, Removal and Installation).
  6. Install the air defector.
    Refer to: Engine Undershield (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
