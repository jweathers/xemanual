Side Panel Sheet Metal Repairs

B-Pillar Outer Panel (G1785189)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Loading...

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the B-pillar outer panel is installed in conjunction with:

  • Front door
  • Rear door
  • Front seat
  • Rear seat cushion
  • Front safety belt retractor
  • Front scuff plate trim panel
  • Rear scuff plate trim panel
  • Headliner
  • Side air curtain module
  1. The B-pillar outer panel is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements.For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Where breakstem fasteners (BSF) removal is instructed remove fastener shaft with a 4mm punch and remove the head with a 6.5mm drill bit. Clean all the drilled holes to make sure they are free from debris.

    • Where self piercing rivets (SPR's) removal is instructed use either the approved SPR tool or 5.3mm drill bit.

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Front Door (501-03, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Rear Door (501-03, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Front Row Seat (501-10, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Rear Seat Cushion (501-10, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Front Safety Belt Retractor (501-20A, Removal and Installation).
  9. For additional information, refer to: Front Scuff Plate Trim Panel (501-05, Removal and Installation).
  10. For additional information, refer to: Rear Scuff Plate Trim Panel (501-05, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Headliner (501-05, Removal and Installation).
  12. For additional information, refer to: Side Air Curtain Module (501-20B, Removal and Installation).
  13. Release and position the floor carpet to one side.
  14. Release and position the floor noise, vibration and harshness (NVH) material to one side.
  15. Remove the front door striker from the B-pillar.
  16. Remove the upper and lower rear door hinges.
  17. Release and position to one side the wiring harness along the B-Pillar and inner rocker panel.
  18. Remove the rear door hinges.
  19. CAUTION:

    Care should be taken not to cut through into the inner reinforcements.

    Measure, mark and cut the B-pillar where the metal inert gas (MIG) welded butt joints are to be made as indicated.
  20. CAUTION:

    Care should be taken not to cut through into the inner reinforcements.

    Measure, mark and cut the B-pillar where the MIG welded butt joints are to be made as indicated.
  21. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  22. NOTE:

    Avoid where possible damaging the NVH components located behind the rocker panel.

    Carefully separate the joints and remove the B-pillar outer panel also releasing the NVH components as indicated.
Installation
CAUTION:

Where structural adhesive is to be applied between surfaces it is essential compression is applied to the joints during the curing period.

NOTES:

  • BSF installation involves using the Jaguar approved BSF installation tool and requires a hole diameter of 6.5mm.

  • SPR installation requires using the Jaguar approved SPR tool.

  1. Remove the rivet remnants.
  2. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  3. Using the old B-pillar outer panel for reference measure, mark and cut the new B-pillar outer panel where the MIG welded butt joint are to be made as indicated.
  4. Offer up the new B-pillar outer panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  5. Drill holes where the BSF's and MIG plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  6. Drill holes where the backing plates are to be installed in the vehicle as indicated.
  7. Drill holes where the backing plates are to be installed in the new B-pillar outer panel as indicated.
  8. Remove the new B-pillar outer panel.
  9. Deburr the drilled holes.
  10. Measure and cut three 50mm wide backing plates, 25mm each side of the MIG butt joint from the B-pillar outer panel remnant.
  11. Cut six run on/run off tabs from the B-pillar outer panel remnant.
  12. Using a fine bristle disc clean and prepare the panel surfaces including the NVH components.
  13. MIG plug weld the backing plates to the vehicle as indicated.
  14. Apply the coupling agent where the Jaguar recommended bonding material is to be applied and allow to dry.
  15. NOTE:

    Make sure a continuous bead of adhesive surrounds fixing holes. Do not apply adhesive in the vicinity of the MIG welds.

    Apply a 5mm zig zag bead of the Jaguar recommended bonding material to the B-pillar outer panel and the NVH components as indicated.
  16. Offer up the new B-pillar outer panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  17. Tack MIG weld the butt joints as indicated.
  18. Install the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  19. Install the BSF's as indicated.
  20. Install the SPR's as indicated.
  21. Install the run on/off tabs and MIG weld the butt joints as indicated.
  22. Dress all welds.
  23. Remove any excess adhesive.
  24. Cut off the run on/run off tabs.
  25. Carry out a non destructive crack inspection on the MIG butt joints. If correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  26. Install the rear door hinges and tighten the bolts to 30Nm.
  27. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  28. Make sure that all installed SPR's are sealed using a Jaguar recommended sealer.
  29. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  30. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
