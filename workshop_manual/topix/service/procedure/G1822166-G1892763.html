Removal
WARNING:

To avoid accidental deployment and possible personal injury, the backup power supply must be depleted before repairing or replacing any air bag supplemental restraint system (SRS) components. To deplete the backup power supply energy, disconnect the battery ground cable and wait one minute. Failure to follow this instruction may result in personal injury.

NOTE:

Removal steps in this procedure may contain installation details.

  2. Disconnect the battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01, General Procedures).
  3. WARNING:

    Make sure to support the vehicle with axle stands.

Installation
  1. CAUTIONS:

    • The alignment mark on the pedestrian impact sensor tube should be aligned with the center of the bumper.

    • The pedestrian impact sensor tube must not be stretched during installation, so that the exposed length of tube at each end is not excessively long.

    • The pedestrian impact sensor tube must not be compressed during installation, so that the exposed length of tube at each end is not too short.

    • Make sure that there are no kinks in the pedestrian impact sensor tube after installation and the exposed length of tube is equal on both sides.

    • Inspect the pedestrian impact sensor for any signs of damage, such as cuts or slits. Install a new component if required.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
