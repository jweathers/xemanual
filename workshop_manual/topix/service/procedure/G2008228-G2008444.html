Removal
CAUTION:

Damage can be caused to the camshaft if it is not locked with a suitable spanner whilst removing the Variable Camshaft Timing (VCT) actuator bolt.

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
  2. WARNING:

    Make sure to support the vehicle with axle stands.

    Raise and support the vehicle.
    • Remove the timing chain.
      Refer to: Timing Chain (303-01B Engine - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  4. NOTE:

    This step requires the aid of another technician.

    • Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1600
    • Make sure the camshaft locking tool is correctly inserted.
    • Using a suitable spanner, lock the intake camshaft.
  5. NOTE:

    This step requires the aid of another technician.

    • Hold the intake camshaft with a spanner and remove the VCT actuator bolt.
    • Discard the bolt.
    • Remove the VCT actuator.
    • Remove and discard the sealing washer.
Installation
  1. CAUTION:

    Make sure that the mating faces are clean and free of foreign material.

    • Install the VCT actuator.
    • Install a new sealing washer.
      Renew Part: Camshaft sprocket friction washer(s) Quantity: 1.
  2. CAUTION:

    Make sure that a new bolt is installed.

    • Install the new bolt by hand.
      Renew Part: Variable camshaft timing actuator bolts Quantity: 1.
  3. NOTE:

    This step requires the aid of another technician.

    • Hold the intake camshaft with a spanner while tightening the VCT actuator bolt.
    • Torque: 72 Nm
  4. Install the timing chain.
    Refer to: Timing Chain (303-01B Engine - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  5. Reconnect the battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
