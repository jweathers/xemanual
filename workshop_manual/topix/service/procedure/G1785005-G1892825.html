Spare Wheel Well (G1785005)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the spare wheel well is installed in conjunction with:

  • Rear bumper
  • Rear armature
  • Battery
  • Rear bumper mounting
  • Loadspace trim panel
  • Back panel outer
  • Back panel inner
  • Exhaust system
  • Fuel tank
  • Rear subframe
  1. The spare wheel well is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements.For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

    • Make sure a zinc rich primer is applied to any bare metal surfaces.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper (501-19, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Rear Armature (501-19, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Battery (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper Mounting (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Left Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation) / Right Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  9. For additional information, refer to: Back Panel Outer (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  10. For additional information, refer to: Back Panel Inner (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Exhaust System - Vehicles Without: Diesel Exhaust Fluid (309-00B Exhaust System - INGENIUM I4 2.0L Diesel, Removal and Installation) / Exhaust System (309-00C Exhaust System - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation) / Exhaust System (309-00A Exhaust System - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  12. For additional information, refer to: Fuel Tank (310-01C Fuel Tank and Lines - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol , Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  13. For additional information, refer to: Rear Subframe (502-00, Removal and Installation).
  14. Release and position the floor carpet to one side.
  15. Release and position the floor noise, vibration and harshness (NVH) material to one side.
  16. Release and position the wiring harness to one side.
  17. Remove the spot welds as indicated.
  18. Carefully separate the joints and remove the spare wheel well.
Installation
  1. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  2. Offer up the new spare wheel well and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  3. Drill holes where the metal inert gas (MIG) plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  4. Remove the spare wheel well.
  5. Deburr the drilled holes.
  6. Clean and prepare the panel surfaces.
  7. Offer up the new spare wheel well and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  8. Install the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  9. Dress the welds.
  10. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  11. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  12. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
