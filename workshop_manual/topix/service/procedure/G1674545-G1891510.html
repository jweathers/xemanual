  3. Rotate the engine clockwise until the crankshaft stops against the special tool as indicated.
  4. CAUTIONS:

    • Make sure that new bolts are installed.

    • Only tighten the bolts finger-tight at this stage.

    Renew Part: Flexplate bolts Quantity: 6.
  5. Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1594
    Torque: 40 Nm
  6. CAUTION:

    Only tighten the bolt finger-tight at this stage.

  7. CAUTION:

    Make sure that the special tool is correctly located.

  8. Torque: 90 Nm
  9. NOTE:

    Tighten the retaining bolts in the sequence shown.

    Torque:
    Stage 1: 50 Nm
    Stage 2: 70 Nm
    Stage 3: 112 Nm
  10. CAUTIONS:

    • Install new friction washers.

    • Note the orientation of the crankshaft sprocket.

    NOTE:

    Install but do not tighten fixing at this stage.

    Renew Part: Crankshaft sprocket friction washer Quantity: 1.
  11. Rotate the engine until the trigger wheel gap and camshafts are positioned as indicated.
  12. Install the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1600
  13. CAUTION:

    Make sure new washers are installed.

    Renew Part: Camshaft sprocket friction washer(s) Quantity: 1.
  14. CAUTIONS:

    • Use an open-ended wrench to hold the camshafts by the hexagon to prevent the camshafts from turning.

    • Make sure that these components are installed to the noted removal position.

    • Only tighten the bolts finger-tight at this stage.

  15. Install the timing chain.
  16. Torque: 10 Nm
  18. Torque: 10 Nm
  19. CAUTION:

    Use an open-ended wrench to hold the camshafts by the hexagon to prevent the camshafts from turning.

    Torque: 72 Nm
  20. CAUTION:

    Using a suitable tool, hold the oil pump gear when installing the centre bolt.

    Torque: 25 Nm
  21. CAUTION:

    Apply sealant WSE-M4G323-A6 in a 4-7mm diameter on the engine front cover as shown. The component must be installed and tightened within 10 minutes of sealant application.

  22. CAUTION:

    Tighten the bolts in the sequence shown.

    Torque:
    M6 10 Nm
    M10 48 Nm
  23. Special Tool(s): 308-511
  24. CAUTIONS:

    • Only tighten the bolt finger-tight at this stage.

    • Make sure that the mating faces are clean and free of foreign material.

    NOTE:

    Apply WSS-M2C913-B or WSS-M2C913-C oil on the damper sealing surface prior to installation.

  25. CAUTIONS:
