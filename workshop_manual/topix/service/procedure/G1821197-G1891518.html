Removal
NOTES:

  • Tool JLR-303-1565 has been superseded by JLR-303-1600, but can still be used to carry out the procedure detailed below.

  • Some variation in the illustrations may occur, but the essential information is always correct.

  • Some illustrations may show the engine removed for clarity.


  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
  2. WARNING:

    Make sure to support the vehicle with axle stands.

    Raise and support the vehicle.
  3. Refer to: Accessory Drive Belt (303-05 Accessory Drive - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  4. Refer to: Starter Motor (303-06 Starting System - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  5. Refer to: Brake Vacuum Pump - GTDi 2.0L Petrol (206-07 Power Brake Actuation, Removal and Installation).
  6. Refer to: Fuel Pump (303-04 Fuel Charging and Controls - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  7. Refer to: Valve Cover (303-01 Engine - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  9. NOTE:

    Using a suitable spanner, lock the camshaft.

    Remove the brake vacuum pump adaptor from the camshaft.
  10. Remove the spanner from the camshaft.
  11. Rotate the engine until the trigger wheel gap and camshafts are positioned as illustrated.
  12. Carefully move the camshafts and install the special tool. The camshaft ends must be positioned as illustrated.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1600
    Torque: 10 Nm
  13. Install the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1594
  14. CAUTION:

    The crankshaft damper bolt and washer cannot be reused.

Installation
  1. CAUTIONS:

    • Only tighten the bolt finger-tight at this stage.

    • Make sure that the mating faces are clean and free of foreign material.

    NOTE:

    Apply WSS-M2C913-B or WSS-M2C913-C on the damper sealing surface prior to installation.

  2. Rotate the crankshaft damper to the position indicated.
  3. Install the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1595
  4. Torque:
    Stage 1: 100 Nm
    Stage 2: 90°
  5. Remove the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1595
  6. Remove the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1600
  7. NOTE:

    Loosen the bolt (1) remove the bolts (2).

    Remove the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1594
  8. NOTE:

    Using a suitable spanner, lock the camshaft.

    Install the brake vacuum pump adaptor to the camshaft.
    Torque: 62 Nm
  9. Remove the spanner from the camshaft.
  10. CAUTION:

    Apply sealant WSK-M2G348-A2 in a 1-1.5mm diameter on the cylinder head and camshaft caps as shown. The components must be installed and tightened within 60 minutes of sealant application.

  11. Torque:
    Stage 1: 7 Nm
    Stage 2: 16 Nm
  12. Refer to: Valve Cover (303-01 Engine - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  13. Refer to: Fuel Pump (303-04 Fuel Charging and Controls - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  14. Refer to: Brake Vacuum Pump - GTDi 2.0L Petrol (206-07 Power Brake Actuation, Removal and Installation).
  15. Refer to: Starter Motor (303-06 Starting System - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  16. Refer to: Accessory Drive Belt (303-05 Accessory Drive - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  17. Connect the battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
