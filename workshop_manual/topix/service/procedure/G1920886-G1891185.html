CAUTION:

Nuts and bolts must be tightened with vehicle at normal ride height.

NOTES:

Installation
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
  2. Using only four-wheel alignment equipment approved by Jaguar, check the wheel alignment, and adjust if required.
