Removal
NOTES:

  • Tool JLR-303-1565 has been superseded by JLR-303-1600, but can still be used to carry out the procedure detailed below.

  • Some variation in the illustrations may occur, but the essential information is always correct.

  • Engine shown removed for clarity.


  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
  2. WARNING:

    Make sure to support the vehicle with axle stands.

    Raise and support the vehicle.
  3. Refer to: Timing Chain (303-01B Engine - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  4. CAUTIONS:

    • Use an open-ended wrench to hold the camshafts by the hexagon to prevent the camshafts from turning.

    • Note the fitted position of the component prior to removal.

  5. NOTE:

    Discard the washers.

  6. NOTE:

    Note the orientation of the component prior to removal.

  7. Remove the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1600
  8. CAUTIONS:

    • Take extra care not to damage the component.

    • Do not lever the camshafts.

    NOTE:

    Note the orientation of the component prior to removal.

  9. NOTE:

    Make sure that all traces of the old sealant are removed from the mating faces.

  10. NOTE:

    Note the orientation of the component prior to removal.

Installation
  1. CAUTION:

    Make sure that these components are installed to the noted removal position.

    NOTE:

    Lubricate the camshafts and the camshaft bearing caps with oil meeting Jaguar specification prior to installation.

  2. CAUTIONS:

    • Make sure that the camshafts and camshaft bearing caps are installed in their original locations.

    • Make sure that the piston No. 1 is at top dead center (TDC) before installing the camshafts.

    • Take extra care not to damage the component.

    NOTE:

    Tighten the retaining bolts in the sequence illustrated.

    Torque:
    Stage 1: 7 Nm
    Stage 2: 16 Nm
  3. CAUTION:

    Apply sealant WSK-M2G348-A2 in a 1-1.5mm diameter on the cylinder head and camshaft caps as shown. The components must be installed and tightened within 60 minutes of sealant application.

    NOTE:

    Lubricate the camshafts and the camshaft bearing caps with oil meeting Jaguar specification prior to installation.

  4. Torque:
    Stage 1: 7 Nm
    Stage 2: 16 Nm
  5. Rotate the camshafts to position them as illustrated.
  6. Install the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): JLR-303-1600
  7. CAUTION:

    Make sure that these components are installed to the noted removal position.

    Torque: 10 Nm
  8. CAUTION:

    Make sure new washers are installed.

    Renew Part: Camshaft sprocket friction washer(s) Quantity: 1.
  9. CAUTIONS:

    • Use an open-ended wrench to hold the camshafts by the hexagon to prevent the camshafts from turning.

    • Note the fitted position of the component prior to removal.

    • Only tighten the bolts finger-tight at this stage.

  10. Install the timing chain.
  11. Torque: 10 Nm
  14. CAUTION:

    Use an open-ended wrench to hold the camshafts by the hexagon to prevent the camshafts from turning.

    Torque: 72 Nm
  15. Refer to: Timing Cover (303-01B Engine - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  16. Connect the battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
