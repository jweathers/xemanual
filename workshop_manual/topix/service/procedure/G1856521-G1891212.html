Removal
NOTES:

  • If a new knuckle is to be installed, a new wheel bearing will be required.

  • Some variation in the illustrations may occur, but the essential information is always correct.

  • Some components shown removed for clarity.


  1. Deactivate the electronic park brake.
    Refer to: Electric Park Brake Service Mode Activation and Deactivation (206-05 Parking Brake and Actuation, General Procedures).
  2. Raise and support the vehicle.
    Refer to: Lifting (100-02 Jacking and Lifting, Description and Operation).
  3. Remove the wheel and tire.
    Refer to: Wheel and Tire (204-04 Wheels and Tires, Removal and Installation).
  4. CAUTION:

    Do not use air tools to remove the halfshaft nut.

    NOTE:

    Retain the nut for use in the installation procedure.

  5. CAUTION:

    Do not use excessive force to remove the component.

  6. CAUTIONS:

    • Discard the bolts.

    • Do not allow the brake caliper to hang on the brake hose.

  7. WARNING:

    Using a suitable stand, support the lower arm.

    CAUTION:

    Discard the nut and bolt.

  8. CAUTION:

    Discard the nut and bolt.

  9. CAUTION:

    Discard the nuts and bolts.

  10. NOTE:

    Discard the nuts and bolts.

  11. Special Tool(s): 205-857
  12. WARNING:

    The component(s) may be heavy. Take extra care when handling.

    CAUTION:

    Discard the bolt.

    NOTE:

    Do not disassemble further if the component is removed for access only.
