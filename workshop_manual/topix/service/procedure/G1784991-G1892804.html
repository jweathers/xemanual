Side Panel Sheet Metal Repairs

A-Pillar Outer Panel (G1784991)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the A-pillar outer panel is installed in conjunction with:

  • Front bumper
  • Hood
  • Front fender
  • Fender splash shield
  • Front door
  • Instrument panel
  • Windshield
  • Headliner
  • Side air curtain module
  • Front scuff plate trim panel
  • A-pillar trim panel
  • Front seat
  1. The A-pillar outer panel is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements.For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Where breakstem fasteners (BSF) removal is instructed remove fastener shaft with a 4mm punch and remove the head with a 6.5mm drill bit. Clean all the drilled holes to make sure they are free from debris.

    • Where self piercing rivets (SPR's) removal is instructed use either the approved SPR tool or 5.3mm drill bit.

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Front Bumper (501-19, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Hood (501-02, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Fender (501-02, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Fender Splash Shield (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Front Door (501-03, Removal and Installation).
  9. For additional information, refer to: Instrument Panel Console (501-12 Instrument Panel and Console, Removal and Installation) / Instrument Panel Lower Trim Panel (501-12 Instrument Panel and Console, Removal and Installation).
  10. For additional information, refer to: Windshield (501-11, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Headliner (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  12. For additional information, refer to: Side Air Curtain Module (501-20 Supplemental Restraint System, Removal and Installation).
  13. For additional information, refer to: Front Scuff Plate Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  14. For additional information, refer to: A-Pillar Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  15. For additional information, refer to: Front Row Seat (501-10 Seating, Removal and Installation).
  16. Release and position the floor carpet to one side.
  17. Release and position the floor noise, vibration and harshness (NVH) material to one side.
  18. Release and position the wiring harness to one side.
  19. Remove the upper and lower front door hinges from the A-pillar outer panel.
  20. CAUTION:

    Care should be taken not to cut through into the inner reinforcements.

    Mark out the positions where the A-pillar outer panel upper metal inert gas (MIG) butt joint is to be made. Cut through the panel as indicated.
  21. CAUTION:

    Care should be taken not to cut through into the inner reinforcements.

    Mark out the positions where the A-pillar outer panel lower MIG butt joint is to be made. Cut through the panel as indicated.
  22. Remove SPR's as indicated.
  23. Remove SPR's as indicated.
  24. NOTE:

    Avoid where possible damaging the NVH components located behind the A-pillar outer panel.

    Carefully separate the joints and remove the A-pillar outer panel also releasing the NVH components as indicated.
Installation
CAUTION:

Where structural adhesive is to be applied between surfaces it is essential compression is applied to the joints during the curing period.

NOTES:

  • BSF installation involves using the Jaguar approved BSF installation tool and requires a hole diameter of 6.5mm.

  • SPR installation requires using the Jaguar approved SPR tool.

  1. Remove the rivet remnants.
  2. Dress flanges where necessary.
  3. Offer up the new A-pillar outer panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  4. Using the old A-pillar outer panel as a guide mark out the position on the new A-pillar outer panel where the upper and lower MIG butt joints are to be made. Cut the new panel as indicated.
  5. Offer up the new A-pillar outer panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  6. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  7. Drill holes where the BSF's and MIG plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  8. Drill holes where the backing plates are to be installed in the upper A-pillar butt joint as indicated.
  9. Drill holes where the backing plates are to be installed in the lower A-pillar butt joint as indicated.
  10. Remove the new A-pillar outer panel.
  11. Deburr the drilled holes.
  12. Measure and cut two 50mm wide backing plates, 25mm each side of the MIG butt joint from the A-pillar outer panel remnant.
  13. Cut four run on/run off tabs from the A-pillar outer panel remnant.
  14. Using a fine bristle disc clean and prepare the panel surfaces including the NVH components.
  15. Install and MIG plug weld the A-pillar upper backing plate as indicated.
  16. Install and MIG plug weld the A-pillar lower backing plate as indicated.
  17. NOTES:

    • Make sure that the SPR's are installed before the installation of the A-pillar outer panel.

    • When the A-pillar outer panel is installed, make sure that the area around the SPR's is clamped until the bonding material has cured.

    Install the SPR's as indicated.
  18. Apply the coupling agent where the Jaguar recommended bonding material is to be applied and allow to dry.
  19. NOTES:

    • Make sure a continuous bead of adhesive surrounds fixing holes.

    • Do not apply adhesive in the vicinity of the MIG plug welds.

    Apply a 5mm zig zag bead of the Jaguar recommended bonding material as indicated.
  20. NOTES:

    • Make sure a continuous bead of adhesive surrounds fixing holes.

    • Do not apply adhesive in the vicinity of the MIG plug welds.

    Apply a 5mm zig zag bead of the Jaguar recommended bonding material to the NVH components as indicated.
  21. Offer up the new A-pillar outer panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  22. Tack MIG weld the upper butt joint as indicated.
  23. Tack MIG weld the lower butt joint as indicated.
  24. Install the BSF's and the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  25. Install the SPR's as indicated.
  26. Install the SPR's and the BSF's as indicated.
  27. MIG plug weld the upper backing plate as indicated.
  28. MIG plug weld the lower backing plate as indicated.
  29. Install the upper run on/off tabs and MIG weld the butt joint as indicated.
  30. Install the lower run on/off tabs and MIG weld the butt joint as indicated.
  31. Cut off the run on/run off tabs.
  32. Dress all welds.
  33. Remove any excess adhesive.
  34. Carry out a non destructive crack inspection on the MIG butt joints. If correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  35. Install the front door upper and lower door hinges and tighten the bolts to 30Nm.
  36. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  37. Make sure that all installed SPR's are sealed using a Jaguar recommended sealer.
  38. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  39. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
