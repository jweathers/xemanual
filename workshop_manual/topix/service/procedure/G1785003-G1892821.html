Rear Floor Side Extension (G1785003)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the rear floor side extension is installed in conjunction with:

  • Rear bumper
  • Rear armature
  • Battery
  • Loadspace trim panel
  • Rear lamp assembly
  • Auxiliary junction box (AJB)
  • Subwoofer amplifier
  • Back panel outer
  • Quarter panel lower extension
  1. The rear floor side extension is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements. For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Where breakstem fasteners (BSF) removal is instructed remove fastener shaft with a 4mm punch and remove the head with a 6.5mm drill bit. Clean all the drilled holes to make sure they are free from debris.

    • Where self piercing rivets (SPR's) removal is instructed use either the approved SPR tool or 5.3mm drill bit.

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

    • Make sure a zinc rich primer is applied to any steel bare metal surfaces.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Rear Armature (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Battery (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation) / Left Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation) / Right Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Rear Lamp Assembly (417-01 Exterior Lighting, Removal and Installation).
  9. NOTE:

    Right hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Rear Junction Box (418-00 Module Communications Network, Removal and Installation).
  10. NOTE:

    Left hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Subwoofer Amplifier (415-01 Information and Entertainment System, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Back Panel Outer (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  12. For additional information, refer to: Quarter Panel Lower Extension (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  13. Remove the spot welds as indicated.
  14. NOTE:

    This spot weld is hidden.

    Remove the spot weld as indicated.
  15. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  16. Carefully separate the joints and remove the rear floor side extension.
Installation
CAUTION:

Where structural adhesive is to be applied between surfaces it is essential compression is applied to the joints during the curing period.

NOTES:

  • BSF installation involves using the Jaguar approved BSF installation tool and requires a hole diameter of 6.5mm.

  • SPR installation requires using the Jaguar approved SPR tool.

  1. Remove the rivet remnants.
  2. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  3. Offer up the new rear floor side extension and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  4. NOTE:

    Make sure the holes are drilled through the side member and the rear floor side extension.

    Drill holes where the metal inert gas (MIG) plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  5. Drill hole for a MIG plug weld as indicated.
  6. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  7. Remove the rear floor side extension.
  8. Deburr the drilled holes.
  9. Clean and prepare the panel surfaces.
  10. Apply the coupling agent where the Jaguar recommended bonding material is to be applied and allow to dry.
  11. NOTES:

    • Make sure a continuous bead of adhesive surrounds fixing holes.

    • Do not apply adhesive in the vicinity of the MIG plug welds.

    Apply a 5mm zig zag bead of the Jaguar recommended bonding material as indicated.
  12. Offer up the new rear floor side extension and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  13. Install the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  14. Install the MIG plug weld as indicated.
  15. Install the BSF's as indicated.
  16. NOTE:

    The components that are located/installed to the weld stud must be offered up and cross checked for alignment prior to the installation of the weld stud to the new panel.

    Prepare the new panel and install the weld stud in its original location as indicated.
  17. Dress the MIG plug welds.
  18. Remove any excess adhesive.
  19. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  20. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  21. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
