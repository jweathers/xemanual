Depressurize
WARNINGS:

  • A small amount of air pressure will be left in the air suspension system.

  • Eye protection must be worn.

  • Make sure the tailgate, hood and all doors are closed.

CAUTION:

Make sure the vehicle is in a clear working area.

  1. WARNING:

    The air suspension is pressurized. Make sure dirt or grease does not enter the system. Always wear hand, eye and ear safety standard protection when working on the system.

    Using the approved Jaguar Land Rover diagnostic equipment, depressurize the air suspension.
      • Follow the on screen prompts to depressurize only the components affected by the repair being undertaken.
Pressurize
  1. Using the approved Jaguar Land Rover diagnostic equipment, pressurize the air suspension.
      • Follow the on screen prompts to pressurize the suspension system.
      • Start and run the engine.
